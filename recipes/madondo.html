MADONDO

This is a Kenyan dish that is loved mainly in the urban surbubs

It is made of beans and other ingredients

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. Boil beans for 1 hour+ till cooked
  2. Rinse the beans with cold water
  3. Wash the onions and tomatoes then cut them into slices
  4. Pour oil in a sufuria and add the ingredients
  5. Add the beans into the sufuria
  6. Let it cook for 10 minutes
  7. Ready to be served, best with Chapati
