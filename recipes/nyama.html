NYAMA WET FRY

This is a Kenyan dish favourite for many people

It is made from beef, mutton or pork

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Meat 1kg
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. Wash the meat and boil for 30 minutes
  2. Add salt
  3. Wash the onions and tomatoes then cut them into slices
  4. Pour oil in a sufuria and add the ingredients
  5. Add the boiled meat into the sufuria
  6. Let it cook for 20 minutes
  7. Ready to be served, best with Ugali
