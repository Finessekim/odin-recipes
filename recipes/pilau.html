PILAU

This is a Kenyan dish favourite for lunch for most people

It is made of rice, meat and other ingredients

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Meat 1kg
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. Wash the meat and boil for 30 minutes
  2. Add salt
  3. Wash the onions and tomatoes then cut them into slices
  4. Pour oil in a sufuria and add the ingredients
  5. Boil rice till cook
  6. Add the boiled meat into the mixture
  7. Let it cook for 30 minutes
  8. Ready to be served, best for lunch
